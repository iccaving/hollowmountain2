\section{Winter Recce, 29-30th Dec 2009}

The trip started, as all good things do, with a 5AM alpine start; we caught the first train out of [townn]{Most Na Soci} and through the Julian alps to [town]{Bohinjska Bistrica}, then a bus to Hotel Zlatorog near [town]{Savica} (the possible Black Sea resurgance for the \passage{Migovec} systems). With ice axe and crampons a quick 1.2km of ascent saw us up on our beloved plateau, with \passage{Tolminski Kuk} brooding in the cloud layer. Based at \passage{Dom na Komni}, we spent the next day in poor visibility logging 5 new blow holes and having a look at the N1-3 entrances.
